This is our project for the seminar L11
Our research question explores the connection between pollen allergy and states or county.
Our hypothesis is that the repartition of pollen allergy is unequal over the territory.
We, therefore, expect to see a significant ANOVA assessing pollen allergy rates between the different states/county.
As explorative, if time allows it,
we will try to produce a map colored by a scale of colour representing the values of the rates of pollen allergy ; 
we will explore the time at which those allergy manifest and if the date differ from one another.
